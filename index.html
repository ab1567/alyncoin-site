🔒 Why AlynCoin?

AlynCoin is a next-generation quantum-secure blockchain built from the ground up for speed, privacy, decentralization, and post-quantum resistance. Originally launched as AbzCoin, it evolved into AlynCoin after years of research and engineering. The project combines zk-STARKs, Falcon & Dilithium signatures, hybrid PoW mining and full GUI/CLI wallet integration to create a new standard in blockchain technology.

We’re not another Ethereum fork. AlynCoin is an entirely custom layer-1 chain, optimized for scalability and built for the future of quantum computing, privacy-first finance, and decentralized governance.
